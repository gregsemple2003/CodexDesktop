# BUG-0009: Primary Action Buttons Are Buried In Scrollable Detail Content

Status: open

## Summary

Primary task actions such as `Dispatch` and `Open Task` are placed at the
bottom of the selected-task detail pane, after long artifact/detail content.
They are not persistently visible while the human is reading the task state.

## Visible Evidence

Human-provided dashboard screenshot on 2026-04-27:

- selected task: `Task-0011`
- the right detail pane is scrollable
- action buttons appear after the artifacts and approval target, near the lower
  edge of the scrollable content
- the human reports that action buttons should be visible at all times and
  should not sit at the bottom of the detail/report panel

## Why This Is A Product Bug

The `Tasks` tab is a command and supervision surface. Primary actions are part
of task state, not an appendix to the artifact list. Burying them inside the
scrolling detail content makes the available action set easy to miss and forces
the human to scroll through report-like content before acting.

## Likely Root Cause

The selected-task detail pane treats actions as just another section in the
scrollable details body instead of a sticky or always-visible command region.

## Expected Behavior

- Primary actions remain visible while the selected task is visible.
- The action region is visually tied to the selected task header or a sticky
  footer/header, not buried below the artifact list.
- Disabled actions remain visible when important, with clear disabled reasons.
- Long artifact lists or bug/report text can scroll independently without
  hiding the task's primary command surface.

## Required Closure Proof

- Actual dashboard-surface evidence shows the selected task with actions visible
  without scrolling to the bottom of the detail pane.
- A long-detail selected task still keeps `Dispatch`, open-context actions, and
  other primary commands visible or predictably sticky.
- Regression evidence checks at least one task whose artifact/detail content is
  long enough to scroll.
